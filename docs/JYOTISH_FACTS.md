### Maitri is ONE-DIRECTIONAL (asymmetric) — the #1 error to avoid
Each planet has its OWN view; read the table ROW-WISE. A pair is "mutual"
ONLY if each appears in the other's row. NEVER write "mutual enemy/friend"
or "the two planets agree" unless both rows match. For a planet placed in a
sign, the dignity that matters is how the **occupying planet** views the
sign's lord (occupant → dispositor); justify from the occupant's side.

Asymmetric pairs that keep getting written wrong:
- **Moon ↔ Mercury:** Mercury regards the Moon as an ENEMY, but the Moon
  regards Mercury as a FRIEND (the Moon has NO enemies). So Cancer is an
  enemy sign *for Mercury*, but the Moon never treats Mercury as an enemy.
- **Moon ↔ Venus:** Venus regards the Moon as an ENEMY, but the Moon regards
  Venus as NEUTRAL. Not mutual.
- **Jupiter ↔ Venus:** Jupiter regards Venus as an ENEMY, but Venus regards
  Jupiter as NEUTRAL. Not mutual.
- **Jupiter ↔ Mercury:** Jupiter regards Mercury as an ENEMY, but Mercury
  regards Jupiter as NEUTRAL. Not mutual.
- **Saturn ↔ Mercury:** Saturn regards Mercury as a FRIEND, but Mercury
  regards Saturn as NEUTRAL. Not mutual.
- **Sun ↔ Mercury:** Sun regards Mercury as NEUTRAL, but Mercury regards the
  Sun as a FRIEND. Not mutual.
- **Moon ↔ Jupiter:** mutual NEUTRAL (Pisces is a neutral sign for the Moon,
  NOT a friend sign).
- Genuinely mutual: Sun↔Venus (mutual enemies); Sun↔Saturn (mutual enemies);
  Mars↔Mercury (Mars enemy of Mercury / Mercury neutral to Mars — NOT mutual,
  check the rows).

## Special planetary aspects (graha drishti)
All planets aspect the **7th**. Additionally:
- **Mars:** 4th, 7th, 8th.
- **Jupiter:** 5th, 7th, 9th.
- **Saturn:** 3rd, 7th, 10th.
(So Jupiter in the 3rd aspects 7th/9th/11th — NOT the 5th.)

## Yogakaraka — strict definition
A planet is yogakaraka ONLY if it owns BOTH a kendra (1,4,7,10) AND a
trikona (1,5,9). **For Meena (Pisces) lagna there is NO single yogakaraka.**
Mars (rules 2nd + 9th) is a strong dharma-lord, NOT a yogakaraka (2nd is not
a kendra). Never call a planet yogakaraka without checking kendra+trikona.

## Upachaya houses
3, 6, 10, 11 (the "growing" houses). Use this exact set.

## No abbreviations (keyword rule)
NEVER write "5L", "4L", "1L+10L", "10L", "5H", "1H/7H". Spell out as
keyword-rich phrases: "the lord of the 5th house", "the lords of the 1st and
10th houses", "the 7th house". Sanskrit on first use (e.g. "panchamesh").

## Exaltation / debilitation

Sun: exalt Aries / debil Libra. Moon: exalt Taurus / debil Scorpio.
Mars: exalt Capricorn / debil Cancer. Mercury: exalt Virgo / debil Pisces.
Jupiter: exalt Cancer / debil Capricorn. Venus: exalt Pisces / debil Virgo.
Saturn: exalt Libra / debil Aries. (Rahu/Ketu: usage varies, avoid hard claims.)

## Digbala (directional strength) by house

1st house → Mercury & Jupiter. 4th house → Moon & Venus.
7th house → Saturn. 10th house → Sun & Mars.
